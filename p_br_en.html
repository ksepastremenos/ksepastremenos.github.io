"Blender Royale" is a community event over at the erindale.xyz discord server. The premise is that all participants get 45 minutes to complete a render while using no pre-built assets. Then a strawpoll is created to pick the best render and fuel some friendly competition. From time to time, the rules are spiced up and there can be elimination rounds, team battles, etc.

This is a collection of renders from the last year and half as I frequented the event. Most renders have been polished after the initial 45' of the event.
