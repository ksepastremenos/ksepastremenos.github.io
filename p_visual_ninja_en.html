A personal game project around thoughts on the institutionalization of creative expression.
The project is playable here (Greek) : simmer.io/@Rigas/visual-ninja

The premise is that you are a university professor and are in the position to pass or fail certain artworks within a given time limit by cutting them in half or letting them pass by.

Then, depending on the amount of students you fail, you are presented with a text, telling you about the way you approached the process and what impact your actions have in the grand scheme of things.

All the artworks are recreations of actual artworks from the university in which I was at the time studying.
