▸ Yet Another Third Lab - Thesis

My Visual and Applied Arts thesis was a two-player, online multiplayer 3D video game.

The game takes place in a virtual environment mimicking the building of the third drawing lab and the navigation is done through the playable characters’ perspective. Inside the game, the players can draw, create structures, write texts and have a break during which they can communicate with each other via microphone. These modes alternate through an in-game timer.
